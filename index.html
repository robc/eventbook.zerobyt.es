Eventbook is the alternative way of quickly accessing your Facebook events when on the go. Ever been caught out trying to remember just where that party was located? Unsure if you RSVP'd to that one big party?

Whether it's by helping you find your way to an event's venue, or even just by letting you check when an event starts whilst you're stuck on an train underground, Eventbook helps make sure you're in the loop on all the details of your social life.

Screenshots

Features

  • View your upcoming FB Events in the next 1, 2 or 4 weeks
  • Filter out those you're not attending
  • Zoom between your location & the event's location on an inline map
  • View the wall & see the latest updates
