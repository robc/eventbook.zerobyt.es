If you've been in that position, then Eventbook is just what you need. Never again worry about being lost on the way to a party, or even arriving too early (or too late).

With a simple tap, Eventbook lets you view all of your events for the next fortnight. Another tap will let you view the finer details of the event, and a third tap will being up the event's location on a map.
